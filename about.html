About Tech & Tidy Services

Reliable, efficient and professional property care across Dubai.

Who We Are

Tech & Tidy Services is a professional maintenance and facility solutions company dedicated to delivering reliable, efficient and high-quality services for homes, offices and villas across the UAE. We combine expertise, innovation and attention to detail to keep every property safe, functional and well-presented.

What We Do

  • General repair & maintenance
  • Home, office & villa renovation
  • Carpentry services & custom woodwork
  • Kitchen renovation
  • Bathroom renovation
  • Home appliances, electrical & plumbing services
  • AC maintenance & repair
  • Painting services
  • Cleaning services & pest / bird control
  • Moving and packing services

Our Promise

We believe every client deserves transparent communication, clear pricing and consistent quality work. Whether it’s a small repair or a full renovation, our team treats every project with the same care and professionalism.
