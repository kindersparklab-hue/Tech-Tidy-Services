Our Services

Complete maintenance, renovation and technical solutions for your home, villa or office.

Repair & Maintenance

We provide complete repair and maintenance solutions to keep your home, villa, or office in excellent working condition. Our qualified professionals handle electrical repairs, plumbing, painting, carpentry, AC issues and general handyman work.

Every job is done with precision using high-quality materials, ensuring long-term safety, comfort and performance.

Home, Office & Villa Renovation

From single-room upgrades to full property makeovers, Tech & Tidy delivers renovation solutions that combine modern design, practicality and quality craftsmanship.

We manage planning, electrical and plumbing work, flooring, painting and finishing, ensuring a smooth and stress-free experience.

Painting Services

We offer interior and exterior painting for homes, offices, villas and commercial spaces. Using premium paints and modern tools, we deliver a smooth, clean and long-lasting finish.

From color consultation to final touch-ups, our team ensures neat, on-time and professional results.

Electrical & Plumbing

Our certified technicians handle wiring, lighting installation, switchboards, power sockets, leak repairs, drainage issues, bathroom and kitchen fittings and more.

We follow strict safety standards and use reliable materials to keep your systems safe, efficient and compliant.

Home Appliance Installation & Maintenance

We provide installation and maintenance for home appliances such as fridges, washing machines and other key equipment, ensuring they are connected and configured correctly.

Our team focuses on safe connection, efficient performance and extended lifespan of your appliances.

Carpentry Services

From custom wardrobes and cabinets to doors, shelves and decorative woodwork, our carpentry services enhance both the look and function of your space.

We combine precise measurements, quality wood and fine finishing to deliver durable and elegant results.

Bathroom Renovation

We remodel bathrooms with new tiles, fixtures, vanities, showers and waterproofing to create modern, comfortable and easy-to-maintain spaces.

Our team designs layouts that make the best use of space while maintaining a clean and stylish look.

Kitchen Renovation

We upgrade kitchens with custom cabinetry, countertops, backsplashes, lighting and plumbing adjustments for better functionality and aesthetics.

Whether you cook daily or occasionally, we create smart, organized and beautiful kitchen environments.

Cleaning Services

Our cleaning team handles deep home cleaning, sofa and upholstery care, carpets, curtains, bathrooms and move-in/move-out cleaning.

We use safe, effective products to ensure a fresh, hygienic and welcoming environment in every room.

AC Maintenance & Repair

We provide AC servicing, deep cleaning, gas checks and reliable repair for split and window AC units to maintain cooling efficiency.

Regular AC maintenance keeps your system running smoothly, reduces breakdowns and improves air quality.

Pest / Bird Control

We offer safe and effective control for insects, rodents and birds, helping protect your property and maintain hygiene.

Our methods focus on safety while achieving long-lasting results, including bird spikes and preventive measures.

Packers & Movers

From packing and loading to transport and unloading, we manage residential and commercial moves with care.

Using proper packing materials and organized planning, we help you move quickly and stress-free.
